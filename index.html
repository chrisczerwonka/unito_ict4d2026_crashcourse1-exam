Sahel Education & Conflict Data Portal

This initiative bridges critical data gaps in Burkina Faso, Mali, and Niger to support education continuity in conflict-affected regions. By layering political violence reports with educational facility infrastructure, our goal is to identify at-risk infrastructure and inform humanitarian intervention strategies.
