Sahel Education & Conflict Data Portal

This portal provides actionable insights on the intersection of political violence and educational infrastructure in Burkina Faso, Mali, and Niger. Our mission is to support humanitarian efforts by identifying at-risk school facilities, bridging data gaps, and informing effective education continuity strategies for students in conflict-affected regions.
